Privacy Policy

Raid IV Odds is an unofficial utility that performs calculations on your device.

The app does not require an account and does not send calculator inputs to a server. Display preferences may be stored locally on your device so the app can remember your theme and view settings.

This build does not load analytics by default. If analytics are added later, they should remain privacy-friendly and should not include your selected Pokemon, entered CP, or IV results.

The public app is a static site. Visitors cannot edit the live app or publish code changes. Public GitHub users may suggest changes through forks or pull requests, but only the repository owner or approved collaborators can merge and deploy them.

Unofficial fan-made calculator. Not affiliated with, sponsored by, endorsed by, or approved by Niantic, Scopely, The Pokemon Company, Nintendo, Creatures, or GAME FREAK.
